Who are we?
Educational Trust works to close opportunity gaps that disproportionately affect students from low-income families.Through our research,we support efforts that expand excellence in education from preschool through college,increase college access and completion particularly for historically underserved students,engage diverse communities dedicated to education.
What we do?
We help deprived children and young adults escape a background of poverty,slum life,illiteracy and ignorance thereby creating opportunities for their integration into mainstream society.The ultimate goal is to provide knowledge and skills for the participants.Our vision is to make a contribution toward the elimination of urban slums and rural poverty.
